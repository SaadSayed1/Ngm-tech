Story Overview

After a violent storm destroys their ship, a lone survivor washes ashore on a mysterious island unlike any they’ve seen before. The island's sands whisper riddles in an ancient, ethereal voice, revealing cryptic messages about the survivor’s past, present, and possible future. As they explore the island, they encounter other strange phenomena—shifting landscapes, ghostly figures, and remnants of civilizations long gone. To escape, the survivor must solve the riddles, but each answer comes at a cost, forcing them to confront their deepest fears and regrets.
