Delivery and Shipping

Home > Delivery and Shipping

Last updated: 12th November 2024

At NGM Tech Growth, we are committed to delivering your books as quickly and safely as possible. Whether you're ordering a single book or multiple titles, we strive to ensure a smooth, efficient, and reliable shipping experience.

Shipping Policy

We offer a variety of shipping options to cater to your needs, including standard and expedited shipping methods. Please review the details of our shipping services below:

  • Standard Shipping: Orders are typically processed within 1–3 business days and arrive within 5–7 business days.
  • Expedited Shipping: If you're in a hurry, we offer expedited shipping for faster delivery. Expedited orders are processed within 1 business day and typically arrive in 2–3 business days.
  • International Shipping: We also offer international shipping to many countries. Shipping times and fees will vary depending on your location.

Order Processing Times

Orders are usually processed and shipped within 1–3 business days after receiving your order. Orders placed on weekends or holidays will be processed on the next business day. You will receive an email with tracking information once your order has shipped.

Shipping Rates

Shipping fees depend on your location, the size of your order, and the shipping method selected. For most standard domestic orders, we offer a flat rate shipping fee. International shipping rates will vary by country.

Track Your Order

Once your order has been dispatched, you will receive an email with a tracking number that you can use to monitor your shipment's status.

Shipping Restrictions

  • We currently do not ship to P.O. Boxes.
  • Some items may not be eligible for shipping to certain regions due to restrictions or availability.
  • International orders may be subject to customs duties and taxes. The customer is responsible for any additional fees imposed by the destination country.

Book Delivery

Once your purchase is complete, your book will be delivered to the email address you provided, within 24 hours. Thank you for your patience!

Delivery Issues

If you encounter any issues with delivery, such as a missing or damaged item, please contact us at info@ngmtechgrowth.com. We will investigate and resolve the issue as quickly as possible.

Returns and Exchanges

For information on how to return or exchange books, please refer to our Return and Refund Policy page. If your order arrives damaged or if there is an issue with your book(s), we will assist you in making the return process as seamless as possible.
